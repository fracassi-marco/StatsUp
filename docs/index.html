Features

Everything Strava doesn't show you

StatsUp goes beyond the basics — giving you the analytics tools that serious athletes need, without the subscription.

📊

Deep Statistics

Monthly and annual breakdowns of distance, elevation, duration, and pace. See trends over time with beautiful charts.

🏅

Achievement Badges

Earn 30+ badges for milestones like running 100km in a month, completing the Everest challenge, or your first marathon.

⚡

Personal Records

Track your all-time bests: max distance, top speed, highest elevation, best pace, and race predictions using Riegel's formula.

❤️

HR Zone Analysis

Visualize how you distribute your effort across 5 heart rate zones. Understand if you're training at the right intensity.

🗺️

Route Maps

View your activity routes on interactive Google Maps. See all your routes on a single map to visualize your training geography.

🎯

Goal Tracking

Set monthly distance and frequency goals. Get smart suggestions based on your historical performance. Never miss a target.

🔖

Bookmarks

Save your favorite workouts with custom notes and difficulty ratings. Build a personal library of memorable activities.

📤

Data Export & Backup

Export all your data to JSON for full portability. Restore anytime. Your data is always yours — stored locally on your device.

🌙

Light, Dark & System Theme

Beautiful in any light. Switch between themes manually or let the system decide. Available in English, Italian, Spanish, and French.
